About Us.

Thanks for visiting Trailcam Data!

Like any great startup, Trailcam Data was founded with a mission to solve a problem that the founders faced. It just so happens, our startup was founded by three brothers who were spending countless hours reviewing images from our trailcams to get the most out of the hunt. We wrote numbers on excel sheets and tried to make sense out of it all.

That's when we took our programming knowledge and tried a to find a way to make it all a bit easier. Like any passion project, we spent countless hours of coding and testing to make something that really made a difference. Our results so far have been amazing, and we're making the software available to everyone in 2018.

We hope you're just as excited about our startup as we are, and hope you sign up. You can follow our updates by signing up on the front page or by following us on our social media channels.
